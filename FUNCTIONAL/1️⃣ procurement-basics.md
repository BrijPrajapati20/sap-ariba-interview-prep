# 📘 Procurement Basics

## ❓ What is Procurement?
Procurement is the process of acquiring goods and services from external suppliers.

---

## ❓ What is PR (Purchase Requisition)?
Request raised by user to procure goods/services.

---

## ❓ What is PO (Purchase Order)?
Official document sent to supplier after approval.

---

## ❓ What is Invoice?
Document submitted by supplier for payment.

---

## ❓ What is 3-Way Matching?
Matching between:
- PO  
- Goods Receipt  
- Invoice  

---

Always explain with real example in interview.
